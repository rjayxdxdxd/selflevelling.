Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the BMI Calculator?

The BMI (Body Mass Index) Calculator helps you estimate your body fat based on your height and weight. It is a useful tool to assess whether you are underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

2. How does the Calorie Calculator work?

The Calorie Calculator estimates the number of calories you need daily based on your age, gender, weight, height, and activity level, helping you manage your diet effectively.

3. Can I get personalized workout plans?

Yes, our website offers workout plans tailored to your BMI and calorie needs to help you achieve your fitness goals.

4. How do I track my progress?

By logging into your account, you can save your assessments, view your BMI and calorie records, and get personalized recommendations over time.

5. Is my personal data safe?

We prioritize your privacy and use secure methods to protect your personal information. Please refer to our privacy policy for more details.

6. What if I have medical conditions?

Our assessment tool politely informs you if certain exercises are not recommended and suggests alternative diets or workouts tailored for your health conditions.
