Nutrition & Diet Recommendations

Choose the activity level that best fits your daily routine. Below each category you'll find examples of typical activities and estimated daily calorie intake.

Sedentary

Little or no exercise, mostly sitting during the day.

Examples: Desk job, watching TV, light household chores like dusting.

Estimated Calorie Intake: 1,600 - 2,000 kcal/day

Lightly active

Light exercise or sports 1-3 days per week.

Examples: Walking 30 min/day, light gardening, casual bike rides.

Estimated Calorie Intake: 1,800 - 2,200 kcal/day

Moderately active

Moderate exercise or sports 3-5 days per week.

Examples: Gym workouts, brisk walking, recreational sports like tennis or swimming.

Estimated Calorie Intake: 2,000 - 2,600 kcal/day

Very active

Hard exercise or sports 6-7 days per week.

Examples: Intense gym sessions, running, competitive sports like soccer or basketball.

Estimated Calorie Intake: 2,400 - 3,000 kcal/day

Super active

Very hard exercise & physical job or training twice per day.

Examples: Professional athletes, heavy manual labor, twice daily training sessions.

Estimated Calorie Intake: 3,000+ kcal/day
