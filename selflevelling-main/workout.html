Underweight (BMI < 18.5)

Recommended Calorie Intake:

  • 2,300–2,800 kcal/day (depending on activity level)

Recommended Activities:

  • Light strength training 2–3x/week
  • Yoga or stretching to improve flexibility
  • Walking or swimming for cardiovascular health
  • High-protein meals to build healthy weight

Normal Weight (BMI 18.5–24.9)

Recommended Calorie Intake:

  • 2,000–2,500 kcal/day

Recommended Activities:

  • Cardio (running, cycling) 3–5x/week
  • Weight training 2–3x/week
  • Yoga or Pilates for flexibility and balance
  • Balanced diet with all food groups

Overweight (BMI 25–29.9)

Recommended Calorie Intake:

  • 1,700–2,000 kcal/day

Recommended Activities:

  • Brisk walking, cycling, or swimming 5x/week
  • Strength training to increase metabolism
  • Focus on whole grains and vegetables
  • Minimize sugary and fried foods

Obese (BMI ≥ 30)

Recommended Calorie Intake:

  • 1,500–1,800 kcal/day (supervised by a health provider)

Recommended Activities:

  • Low-impact exercises (e.g., chair workouts, swimming)
  • Gentle walking, progressing to longer durations
  • Consult with a professional before high-intensity activity
  • Focus on portion control and hydration
